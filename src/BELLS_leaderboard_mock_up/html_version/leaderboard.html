Overall Ranking

A comprehensive comparison of safeguard performance across key metrics: Detection Rate for both adversarial and non-adversarial content, False Positive Rate, and the overall BELLS Score. The color-coding system helps quickly identify performance levels across different metrics.

  • LLM Guard leads overall with the highest BELLS Score, showing balanced performance across all metrics
  • Top performers maintain Detection Rates above 90% while keeping False Positives low
  • Notable trade-off between Detection Rate and False Positives among lower-ranked safeguards

Performance by Category

Detailed breakdown of safeguard effectiveness across different harm categories, based on non-adversarial harmful prompts only. The heatmap visualization highlights strengths and specializations of each solution, making it easy to identify which safeguards excel in specific areas of protection. Note that this evaluation focuses on straightforward harmful content without sophisticated evasion techniques.

  • Strong performance across most safeguards in detecting explicit harm categories
  • More variation in effectiveness for nuanced categories like disinformation
  • Some safeguards show specialization in specific harm types

Jailbreak Type Analysis

This visualization breaks down each safeguard's effectiveness against different types of jailbreak attempts. Click on individual bars to explore detailed performance breakdowns for specific jailbreak methods within each category.

  • Most safeguards show higher detection rates for sophisticated jailbreak attempts
  • Top performers maintain consistent detection rates across different jailbreak types
  • Some safeguards show specialized strength in detecting specific jailbreak patterns
